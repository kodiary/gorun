Wednesday, 08 June 2016

Happy Global Exhibition Day!

 

 

Today is Global Exhibition Day  and we are celebrating the dynamic industry that we are part of, the value that exhibitions bring to the economy, the jobs that they create and the people that they bring together.


Thank you to all that attended the networking breakfast event at the Johannesburg Expo Centre this morning, where the EXSA Academy was officially launched and industry thought leaders shared valuable insights.


Dave Nemeth’s 6 Trend points; taking inspiration from the past, making your brand and product relevant, authenticity, creating an experience, utilizing future technology, and disruptive interpretation, provided attendees with tips on how to design differently. Nonnie Kubeka encouraged the industry to celebrate what they bring to the economy through innovation and to become trend setters and not followers. While, Gift Luthuli reminded attendees that things can go wrong so it imperative that the Industry understands, implements and follows all the necessary health and safety procedures.

 

“Today the inaugural Global Exhibition Day was a resounding success and we look forward to celebrating and growing the event in the years to come. . Thank you to our sponsors Scan Display, Expo Centre Nasrec, Main Event Catering, Compex and LARouxnelle Logistics and Consulting and all that attended for making the event a success” says Phumulani Hlatshwayo, EXSA’s newly appointed General manager!

 

Global Exhibitions Day is an initiative driven by UFI – The Global Association of the Exhibition Industry and the International Association of Exhibitions and Events™ (IAEE) and supported by AEO, that aims to promote the importance of exhibitions for global business by showcasing innovation and facilitating international trade.
