Britain Votes to Leave European Union: UFI Issues Statement on Global Exhibition Industry Impact

 

 

Despite polls indicating that the opposite result would be achieved, Britain has voted to leave the European Union, which it has been a member of since the mid-1970s. The vote's impact on the British pound and stock markets worldwide was swift, with the U.K. currency dropping to a 30-year low and the FTSE 100 also seeing steep declines. The exhibition industry is a global one, reaching the far corners of the earth, so certainly the British decision to exit the European Union will have an impact.
