Roger Simons to headline the EGF 2016 Conference
 

View this email in your browser

Breaking news

21 July 2016
Hackle Brooke Conference Centre

The Event Greening Forum is delighted to haveRoger Simons,Regional Sustainability Director at MCI and the President of the Green Meetings Industry Council (GMIC), as the keynote speaker for the EGF 2016 conference.

Simons has over 14 years’ experience in designing and delivering winning sustainability strategies and organising sustainable events. He is recognised internationally as a thought leader on sustainability issues and will share global insights into how brand communications strategies are evolving in the 21st century, and how events are increasingly an important component of major brands' sustainability strategies.  

Simons will also be hosting a Sustainable MICE Excellence Master Class at the SAACI Association Hub on 20 July 2016. The workshop is aimed at venues, convention bureaus and planners.

The workshop will cover: 

  • Key leadership trends in MICE
  • Sustainable food
  • ISO20121 – What it is and how it works
  • Insight into communicating your sustainability strategy

Who should attend the EGF 2016 Conference?
Event Suppliers & Services:
Venues; Exhibition Organisers; Transport; AV & Production; Marketing & Communications; Food & Beverages  

Marketing, Procurement & Event Managers:
Corporate; Association & Public sectors
